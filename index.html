What is Open Coffee Club?

OpenCoffee Club is a regular, open and informal meeting place for people involved in startups and/or tech to meet.

The key is a regular place and a regular time - it's not important who comes along, some days it might be no one - just that people know if they want to meet, this is the time and this is the place.

We talk tech, share stories, highlight community events, and help people who need jobs find others who have openings. It's fertile, extra-caffeinated ground for growing relationships within your community.
